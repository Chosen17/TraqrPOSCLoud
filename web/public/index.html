Our mission

We believe hospitality businesses should own their till and their data. Traqr POS runs on hardware you choose. Our cloud is an optional add-on: sync when you want, see orders and menus in one dashboard, and keep full control. No lock-in, no surprise fees.

What we offer

A point-of-sale system that works when the internet doesn’t, plus an optional cloud dashboard so you can see and manage everything in one place.

Till works offline

Your till keeps taking orders and payments even when the internet is down. Data syncs to the cloud when you’re back online. You’re never stuck.

One dashboard for orders & menus

Log in to the cloud portal to see recent orders, sync menus across locations, and manage devices. Void or refund from the dashboard when you need to.

Multi-location ready

One organisation, many stores. Head office can publish menus; each store can override what’s available. Perfect for small chains and franchises.
